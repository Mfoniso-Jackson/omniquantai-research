# Hackathon Demo Script

1. Open the app at `http://127.0.0.1:8000`.
2. Show the product framing: OmniQuantAI turns a portfolio-manager research question into an evidence-backed recommendation in minutes.
3. Run: `Analyse Nvidia as a long-term investment.`
4. Walk through the sections: market evidence, news evidence, macro evidence, evidence table, hypotheses, risks, recommendation, and explanation.
5. Point out the guardrails: no trade execution, no guaranteed returns, human approval required, and explicit counterarguments.
6. Run the scenario: `What if interest rates increase by 1%?`
7. Show the update: macro risk rises, hypothesis probabilities move, and the recommendation changes from BUY to HOLD with tighter sizing.
8. Close with the extension path: replace mock providers with live APIs, add portfolio holdings, add source citations, and export investment committee memos.
